Grounded is a science fiction game where you and your three friends have been shrunk down to the size of insects in the middle of an overgrown suburban backyard set in the 1990s1. zelda

The Legend of Zelda is a captivating saga that spans generations of games. It revolves around the courageous young man, Link, who belongs to the elf-like Hylian race, and the magical princess, Zelda, who hails from the bloodline of the goddess Hylia. Together, they strive to save the enchanting land of Hyrule from the clutches of Ganon, an evil warlord turned demon king and the principal antagonist of the series1.
